What should have been an uneventful trip up the Atlantic coast of Georgia, became a little more interesting when one side of the forward dinghy bridle came off - leaving the dinghy hanging at a precarious angle! Oh yeah, it happened just as we left the river channel and entered the larger waves of the Atlantic!!

But… after a bit of gritted teeth and grumpy words, we sorted it out and resumed our passage Northwards.
