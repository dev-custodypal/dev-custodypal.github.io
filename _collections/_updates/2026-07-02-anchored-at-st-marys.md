Waves were predicted at 3ft, and it was quite lumpy when we left. 

Wind was on the nose, so we left the engines on and motor sailed. 

After about 7 hours we saw dark clouds off to the East and the wind picked up. The gusts hot up to over 21 kts, so we reefed the main. 

When the wind died down to 15 kts we left the reef in as the weather radar showed more wind was heading our way. Eventually the wind came around to the East and gave us a much better point of sail - so we killed the motor and were able to sail!
