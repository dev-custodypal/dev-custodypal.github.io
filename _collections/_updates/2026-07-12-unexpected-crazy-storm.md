---
title: "Unexpected Crazy Storm!"
date: 2026-07-12
---

We were having trouble setting the anchor at Cumberland island so we decided to head back to St Mary’s. Fortunately for us we were hit with an expected thunderstorm with driving rain and wind up to 46 knots!

The wind and the rain were so forceful it was hurting my face! I couldn’t see much beyond the bow of the boat. 

Eventually I turned the boat around and had the stern facing into the wind and waves just so I could see. 

The wind was blowing us so much I had the engine at our normal maximum just to keep still - even then I was fighting a losing battle. As the wind and visibility was so bad I used a channel marker as a reference point to keep us safely in the channel. 

Quite the eventful afternoon!
