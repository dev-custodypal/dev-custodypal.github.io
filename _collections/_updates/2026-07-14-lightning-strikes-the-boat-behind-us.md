We were having dinner when the wind picked up and rain began falling heavily.

Soon the lightning began, and we started counting after each strike to gauge how far away each strike was. 10… 14… 11… the storm felt really close. Each lighting strike felt really bright and each thunderclap felt loud. We huddled around our table and Sage got quite concerned. 

Then we felt that the storm was moving away, the sky started to brighten. 

As we were looking out the back window, all of a sudden, lightning struck the catamaran just behind us!

The photo doesn’t do it justice, but the boat is pretty darn close. And…. That’s where we were parked just the other day!

I don’t think that anyone is onboard the boat - so fingers crossed no one has been hurt.
