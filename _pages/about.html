About Us

Meet the sailors behind the adventure

Our Story

We're Sarah and Mike, and we've been living aboard our 35-foot sailboat "Wanderlust" for the past two years. What started as a crazy dream has become the adventure of a lifetime.

Both of us worked traditional office jobs in the city, but we always felt like something was missing. We spent our weekends dreaming about travel and adventure, watching sailing videos on YouTube, and imagining what life could be like if we just took the leap.

In 2023, we decided to stop dreaming and start doing. We sold most of our possessions, bought a boat, and spent six months learning to sail and preparing for this journey. It wasn't easy, and we had plenty of doubts along the way, but we've never looked back.

Since then, we've sailed thousands of nautical miles, visited dozens of countries, and met incredible people from all walks of life. We've weathered storms, witnessed breathtaking sunsets, and learned more about ourselves than we ever could have in our old lives.

Our Boat - "Wanderlust"

Specifications

  • Length: 35 feet (10.7 meters)
  • Type: Cruising Sloop
  • Year: 1995
  • Home Port: Currently nomadic!

She's not the newest or fanciest boat, but she's sturdy, reliable, and has become our home. We've made countless upgrades and repairs, learning boat systems inside and out.
