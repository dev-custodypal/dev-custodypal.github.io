About Us

Meet the sailors behind the adventure

Our Story

We're Suzanne and Adam, and we moved aboard our 45-foot sailboat "Freddy" in February 2026. We are just at the begining of a crazy dream that started before 2018.

Our Boat - "Freddy"

Specifications

  • Make / Model: Leopard 45
  • Length: 45 feet (13.7 meters)
  • Type: Catamaran
  • Year: 2019

She's not the newest or fanciest boat, but she's sturdy, reliable, and has become our home. We've made countless upgrades and repairs, learning boat systems inside and out.
